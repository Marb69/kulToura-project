About Us

kulToura is a passion project built with a heart for Filipino culture. As a solo creator, I wanted to create a space where our roots, stories, and identity can live on—not just in books or classrooms, but online, accessible to all.

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and celebrate the beauty of Philippine culture through storytelling, visuals, and shared knowledge. From traditional dances to native dishes, kulToura is here to showcase what makes us uniquely Filipino.

Our Vision

A generation that embraces its cultural identity with pride, and continues to pass on our traditions to the next.

Why I Created KulToura

Growing up surrounded by rich stories, colorful festivals, and timeless values, I realized how much of our culture is slowly being forgotten in the digital age. I made kulToura to reconnect not just with my own roots but to help others explore, learn, and appreciate our heritage. This website is more than a project. Let’s not forget who we are.
