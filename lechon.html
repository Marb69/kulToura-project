Lechon

Lechon is a beloved Filipino dish that features a whole roasted pig, cooked over an open flame or in an oven. Known for its crispy skin and tender meat, it is often served at major celebrations like fiestas, weddings, and birthdays. The preparation of lechon is considered an art, with regional variations, including the use of unique marinades and seasonings that reflect local flavors and traditions.
